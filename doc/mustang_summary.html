In summary, the North American P-51 Mustang was an iconic aircraft of the World War II and the Korean War, and it played a key role in escorting Allied bombers on long-range missions over Germany and also used as a fighter-bomber and reconnaissance aircraft. Its exceptional range, speed and maneuverability made it one of the most successful fighters of World War II, and it remained in service until the 1980s. The Mustang is still considered one of the greatest fighters of all time, and continues to be celebrated and admired by aviation enthusiasts around the world.
